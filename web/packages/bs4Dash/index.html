bs4Dash: A 'Bootstrap 4' Version of 'shinydashboard'

Make 'Bootstrap 4' Shiny dashboards. Use the full power of 'AdminLTE3', a dashboard template built on top of 'Bootstrap 4' <https://github.com/ColorlibHQ/AdminLTE>.
